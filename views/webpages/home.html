Candid Capture

Capture the best movements of life


Info

Candid Capture is a tool made to capture the special movements of your lives at home.

Capture from your home

Candid Capture uses IOT with a highly advanced emotion detector to capture fun moments at your home. We use a combination of your environment, audio and emotion to determin when to capture candid photos to brighten up your day.

Features

Comprehensive overview of our functionalities

Emotion Detection

We use the face_api.js to detect your face location and then your expression which can range from joyful, sad to anger and disgust.

Spotify music

With Spotify integration we can play your favourite songs to lighten your mood.

Blur Detection

No one likes a blurry picture. Our blur detection discards images that are blurry.

Voice Command

With our speech to text technology you can give speech commands to take pics on voice command..
